My Web Developement Journey

Curiosity

My Web Developement journey started in High School where it was a part of my IT A Level. I quickly fell in love with the aspect of creating something original from my own imagination. To me, Web Design is digital art and a laptop is the paintbrush. Its the perfect creative outlet for someone who loves computers!

Understanding

I decided to take my interest further and enrolled on an evening College course alongside my full time job. This is where I was first introduced to semantic HTML and CSS. I made my first Website using table cells and Dreamweaver (Yikes!).

Implementation

I then took some online courses in my spare time over the next few months on Codeacademy. My passion and thirst to learn more about the industry led me to enroll in University! I am currently a second year student of Web Design and Development at Southampton Solent University.
